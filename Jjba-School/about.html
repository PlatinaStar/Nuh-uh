The man you see on the picture is called Hirohiko Araki. He has started creating the "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure" manga in 1987 in his homeland - Japan

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a japanese manga with a unique style. At first representing "buff" men as it's main cast, in "part 5", Araki has made the protagonist much slimmer (viz. protagonists tab)

In the first 2 parts, we can see the protagonists using a power called "Hamon", from part 3 onwards, they use power called "Stand"
